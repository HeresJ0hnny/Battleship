
Battleship v1
-Jonathan Shi-

-Window is resizable
-Singleplayer and multiplayer
-5 ships per player

Controls:
-To place:
    -double click on a space for the first end, then click on one of the dark blue squares to place the other end
    -double click the ship to remove
    -press the green arrow when done
    -press Randomize to randomize ship placement
-To shoot:
    -double click on a space: a dark blue square will appear
    -press the green arrow to confirm
    -in multiplayer, the result will be displayed; click anywhere to continue
    -in singleplayer, choose another spot to shoot.
